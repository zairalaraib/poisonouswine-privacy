Privacy Policy — Poisonous Wine

Last updated: June 2025

Summary

Poisonous Wine collects no data whatsoever. It works fully offline and never contacts any server.

What we collect

Nothing. The app stores your game progress (unlocked chapters, best scores, and settings) in your device's local storage only. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Analytics and advertising

The app contains no analytics SDK, no advertising SDK, no crash-reporting SDK, and no third-party libraries that send data anywhere.

Network access

The app makes zero network requests. An internet connection is not required to play.

Children

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children. It is safe for all ages.

Third parties

No data is shared with any third party because no data is collected.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at the email address listed on our Play Store developer page.
